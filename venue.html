Location

Civic Center

Plot GA 1, Ozumba Mbadiwe Ave

Victoria Island, Lagos State, Nigeria

Civic Center in Victoria Island, Lagos, is a prominent, upscale event venue in Nigeria known for its modern design and versatile spaces. Located on Ozumba Mbadiwe Avenue, Civic Center overlooks the Lagos Lagoon, providing a scenic waterfront view and a serene environment for gatherings.
