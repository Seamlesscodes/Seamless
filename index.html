About the Event

The Skill Up Conference is an annual event dedicated to fostering growth, learning, and professional connections in technology. Hosted in collaboration with NITDA and FUTO, this event brings together industry experts, thought leaders, and enthusiasts to explore advancements in fields like AI, blockchain, cybersecurity, and beyond. Join us for a powerful two-day experience designed to elevate your tech journey.
